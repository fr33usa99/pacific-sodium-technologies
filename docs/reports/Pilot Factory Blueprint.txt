**Project Title**: Salton Sea Pilot Facility v1.0  
**Company**: Pacific Sodium Technologies LLC

**Lead**: Adam A. Sanchez, Sole Member & Manager  
**Date**: April 25, 2025

---

### I. Mission
To establish a scalable, fire-safe sodium-ion battery production system powered by geothermal energy and supplied by brine-extracted sodium. The Salton Sea site will serve as a zero-waste, closed-loop demonstration of domestic energy innovation.

---

### II. Facility Overview

**Site Type**: Modular prefabricated structure on leased industrial land  
**Phase**: MVP (Minimum Viable Plant)  
**Size**: ~5,000 sq ft footprint

#### Key Zones:
1. **Brine Intake Bay**  
   - Partnership with existing geothermal operators (BHE Renewables / Controlled Thermal Resources)
   - Secure, filtered inflow pipe from brine return systems

2. **Sodium Extraction Room**  
   - Crystallization and filtration tanks
   - Automated skimmer + membrane purification

3. **Battery-Grade Processing Lab**  
   - Dry-room controlled area for sodium sulfate conversion
   - Bench-scale testing for electrode formulation (NaFePO4, NaTiO, etc.)

4. **Cell Manufacturing Floor**  
   - Coin-cell and pouch cell production line
   - Precision rollers, sealers, electrolyte injection under dry conditions

5. **Reinjection & Water Recycling System**  
   - Waste brine recomposition and re-balancing
   - Monitored reinjection via return pipe into Salton Sea or geothermal basin

6. **Geothermal Heat Loop Integration**  
   - Heat exchangers integrated with adjacent geothermal outlet
   - Provides 80–120°C process energy without external power grid

7. **Control Room & Public Dashboard**  
   - Real-time monitoring of brine chemistry, sodium yield, and environmental metrics
   - Public transparency screens + stakeholder livestream feed

---

### III. Environmental and Energy Compliance
- 100% reinjection of brine effluent
- Closed-loop zero freshwater use
- No lithium, cobalt, nickel, or manganese inputs
- Fully fire-safe sodium-ion design
- Air quality permit: Required
- Brine handling compliance: In coordination with CA Geothermal Resource Boards
